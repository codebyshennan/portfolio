## What it is

firstyearin is a community of stories from people in their first year of something hard: first job, first city, first child, first founder year, first major career shift, or any other beginning that looks cleaner from the outside than it feels on the inside.

## Why it exists

The first year of a major transition is often when people most need context, but most public narratives arrive only after the difficult part has been edited into a neat lesson. firstyearin is meant to preserve the messy middle while it is still useful to someone else.

## Current direction

- Curated first-person essays and anonymous submissions
- Story categories around work, place, family, entrepreneurship, and identity
- Lightweight contributor flow for people who want to share without becoming creators
- A calm reading experience for people looking for signal, not advice theater
